How Elo Works
📊 All players start at Elo 0. You must play at least 1 ranked game to appear on the leaderboard.
📈 Beat a higher-rated player → gain more points. Lose to a lower-rated player → lose more points.
🔒 Only online ranked games affect your Elo. CPU and local games are unranked.
👤 Set your profile to Private to hide from this board — your Elo is still tracked.
